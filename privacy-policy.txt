This application requires ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, ACCESS_WIFI_STATE,
INTERACT_ACROSS_USERS, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_PHONE_STATE,
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and INTERNET permission to
perform its designed functionality, execute user defined scripts on certain
events, e.g., when phone screen connects to a certain WIFI, move certain files
from one folder to another.

This application does not collect or store or transfer any data for any purpose.
